Since 2018, different practices have developed in parallel; design, photography, teaching, and painting. As these activities mutually inform one another, they help expand the notion of having a creative practice that can take on different forms. In 2022 Formafantasma (Milan, IT) opened a second office in Rotterdam as a result of a long time collaboration. The satellite functions as an office with the aim to translate research-driven insights into more commerical projects. The office moved to Middelburg end of 2024. This website shares a selection of works developed since 2015
