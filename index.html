Buttercup, 2024, 896 × 1160 mm, acrylic on canvas

Since 2018, different practices have developed in parallel; design, photography, teaching, and painting. As these activities mutually inform one another, they help expand the notion of having a creative practice that can take on different forms. In 2022 Formafantasma (Milan, IT) opened a second office in Rotterdam as a result of a long-term collaboration. The satellite functions as an office with the aim of translating research-driven insights into more commercial projects. The office moved to Middelburg at the end of 2024. Painting started as a way to materialize memories and connect to visual material with personal meaning. Teaching began in 2017 at BA Design Academy Eindhoven in a department led by Ilse Crawford (StudioIlse) later continued by Thomas Lommée (Open Structures) and expanded in 2022 to the MA Geo-Design programme led by Metahaven. This page shares a selection of works developed since 2015 focussing on design, photography and painting.

Projects in design: Jil Sander, Flos, Cassina, Prada, Friedman Benda, Bvlgari, Tiffany & Co., Quadro, Giustini/Stagetti, La Biennale di Venezia, Sissel Tolaas, Serpentine Galleries, Dzek, NGV Triennale, Assab Collection with Disè, Ferrari, Olympics, Maison Matisse, Kiriyama, Fondation Cartier, Hermès, Hem, CC-Tapis, Tomyships, Calvin Klein.

Projects in photography: Kamakura, Francon, Besnard, Pane, Spring Court, Jerome Clothiers, Pauw, Ficus Shanghai, Berg & Berg.
